Quarterly Downtown District Meetings

Every quarter, members of the three downtown districts are invited to a meeting to hear important news for the district. 

Districts are:

  • Northside District
  • Historic Arts District (Perkins/59 south to Bowery St.)
  • Canal District (Bowery St. south to Bartges St.)

Updates include but are not limited to:

-City of Akron construction projects
-Downtown Akron Partnership updates
-Neighborhood updates
-Special events

The meetings will be held quarterly, the second Thursday of the month. 

Northside District, 1 p.m.,
September 12, 2019, Jilly's Music Room, 111 N. Main St. (one time meeting time change to 1:30 p.m. for September)
December 12, 2019

Historic Arts District, 10 a.m., Akron Summit County Public Library
October 10, 2019

Canal District, 2 p.m., Barley House
August 8, 2019 
November 14, 2019

Additional monthly Main Street meetings (first Thursday of the month)
Barley House (back meeting room), 222 S. Main St., 2:30 p.m.
August 1
September 5
October 3
November 7
December 5

If you are not able to attend the meeting held in your district, you are welcome to attend another district’s meetings. Contact Kimberly Beckett at 330-374-7676 or kbeckett@downtownakron.com if you would like to be added to the meeting invitation list for your district.
