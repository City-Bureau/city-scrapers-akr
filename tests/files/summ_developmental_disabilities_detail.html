Event Type

Event Description

Board Meetings will be held the on the fourth Thursday of each month at 5:30 p.m., unless otherwise noted (*).

Please note that meeting dates, locations, or times are subject to change, and may be confirmed by calling 330-634-8082.

If you would like to be added to Sunshine Law meeting notification list, please contact the Executive Administrative Assistant to the Superintendent, Maggi Albright at 330-634-8082 or MAlbright@SummitDD.org.

    • Summit Co. Probate Court Look Beyond Art Show

    • The Summit County Probate Court will host the Look Beyond Art show exhibit from October 11 through November 1, 2019.

      Join Summit DD and the Probate Court as we highlight artists of all abilities at the Look Beyond Art Show. Various styles and pieces of art created by local artists with developmental disabilities will be on display in the courthouse during normal business hours.

      Artists Reception

      Come meet the artists during the artists reception on Monday, October 28 from 11:30-12:30 p.m. Light snacks and refreshments will be provided.

      Looking to purchase art?  Please contact Beth Yoder for details.
