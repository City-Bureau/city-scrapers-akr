PLANNING | GIS

Coordinating development and growth throughout Summit County and planning for future development and answering questions from the public about development concerns is the function of the Planning Office. The Summit County Planning Commission reviews and approves major and minor subdivisions via the Summit County Subdivision Regulations within the unincorporated areas of Summit County and also provides zoning reviews.
