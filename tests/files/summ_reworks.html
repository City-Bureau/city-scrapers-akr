Scrap Tire Collection at HHWRC Details

Summit County residents can bring tires to the HHWRC during seasonal hours.

Beginning June 6,2019 Summit County residents will be able to drop off unwanted passenger car and light truck sized tires at ReWorks' Household Hazardous Waste Recycling Center (HHWRC.)

Please keep in mind the following:

  • Summit County residents only (no businesses)
  • $1.00 fee per tire (CASH ONLY)
  • 10 tire limit per vehicle (passenger car & light truck size)
  • Tires may be on or off the rim
  • passenger or light truck sized tires only
  • The HHWRC will be closed on Thursday, July 4, 2019 in observation of Independence Day
For more information

Document Shredding and Recycling Event in Akron Details

Saturday October 5, 2019, 9 AM - Noon
Tweet

ReWorks will hold a free document shredding and recycling event Saturday October 5, 2019 at the Betty Jane Community Learning Center located at 444 Darrow Road, in Akron, 44305. The event is from 9:00 a.m. until 12:00 noon.  Please note that Darrow Road is also known as State Route 91 and Betty Jane Community Learning Center is approximately 1 mile south of Tallmadge Circle.

PLEASE NOTE:
> Only Summit County residents and businesses are welcome.
> All paper shredded will be recycled.
> Limit 10 file boxes or shopping bags (please do not tape up boxes; paper bags are preferred over plastic)
> Remove all metal clips, rubber bands and plastic report covers
> STAPLES ARE OKAY

NO HAZARDOUS WASTE WILL BE ACCEPTED AT THIS EVENT.
