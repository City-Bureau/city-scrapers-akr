Board of Directors

Our board members

Our board members serve as representatives of the Summit County community through appointments made by two appointing authorities. The Summit County Executive appoints eight of the Board positions. The Ohio Department of Mental Health and Addiction Services appoints six positions. Members are appointed for a four-year term and may be reappointed for a second four-year term.
